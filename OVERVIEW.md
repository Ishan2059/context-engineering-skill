## 🧠 What is Context Engineering?

> "Context engineering is the delicate art and science of filling the context window with just the right information for the next step." — Andrej Karpathy

**Simple explanation:**
- **Prompt Engineering** = How you ask the question
- **Context Engineering** = What information the AI has access to

Context engineering is the systematic practice of designing, structuring, and optimizing the information provided to AI systems.
